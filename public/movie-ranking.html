The Best Movies according to Tanish

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Taare Zameen Par

Release : 2007
Directors:Amole Gupte, Aamir Khan
Cast:Aamir Khan, Darsheel Safary, Vipin Sharma, Tisca Chopra

It's a masterpiece, the best movie I've watched. The emotions in the story are written and performed so beatifully it touches the deepest corners of your heart.

Soul

Release:2020
Director: Pete Docter
Cast: Jamie Fox, Tina Fey

Another well-written script, soul really makes you reflect on life and your purpose in it.

Dune : Part One

Release: 2021
Director:Denis Villenevue
Cast:Timothee Chalamet, Zendaya, Oscar Isaac, Rebecca Ferguson

This movie is a flawless all-rounder with everything from the story and acting to the music and cinematography done perfectly.
